Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Trading Insights ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

1️⃣ Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Trading Insights:

Account Information:

  • Full name and email address (required for registration)
  • Password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication — never stored in plain text)
  • Profile details you provide during onboarding

Device Information:

  • Device model, OS version, and unique device identifier
  • Used for device registration and license verification
  • App version and installation information

Usage & Activity Data:

  • Trading signals viewed and subscribed to
  • Expert Advisor (EA) download and usage history
  • In-app navigation and feature usage

Payment & Subscription Data:

  • Subscription plan and status
  • Payment transaction history (payment amounts, dates)
  • We do not store credit card or bank account numbers

2️⃣ How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve our services:

  • Authenticate your account and verify your identity
  • Provide trading signals, EA downloads, and subscription features
  • Process subscription payments and manage your plan
  • Send important notifications (signals, app updates, account alerts)
  • Verify device registration for license enforcement
  • Provide customer support via in-app live chat and email
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Share your data with unauthorized third parties

3️⃣ Third-Party Services We Use

We use trusted third-party services to operate our app. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — Authentication and push notifications (FCM). Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services — App delivery and in-app billing on Android. Google Privacy Policy
  • In-App Live Chat — Customer support is handled directly within the app via our built-in live support chat feature.

These services may collect and process your data according to their own policies. We only share the minimum data necessary for these services to function.

4️⃣ Data Storage & Security

Your account credentials are stored securely on Google Firebase servers. Application data is stored on our own secured backend servers. We implement the following security measures:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed — we cannot view your plain-text password
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict unauthorized authentication access
  • API endpoints require authenticated Bearer tokens (JWT)
  • Device registration prevents unauthorized account sharing

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% immune. Please use a strong, unique password for your account.

5️⃣ Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services:

Data Type Retention Period
Account data While your account is active
Transaction records 7 years (legal/accounting compliance)
Device logs Up to 90 days
Deleted account data Removed within 30 days of deletion request

To request account deletion, see the Account Deletion section above, or contact us at support.trading.insights@gmail.com.

6️⃣ Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send you push notifications, including:

  • New trading signals and market alerts

  • Important app announcements
  • Account-related notifications

You can disable push notifications at any time from your device settings. Disabling notifications will not affect your core app functionality.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.
