1️⃣ Information We Collect

1.1 — Account & Registration Data

  • Full name — used to personalize your account
  • Email address — used for login, notifications, and support
  • Password — stored as an encrypted hash via Firebase Authentication; we never store or see your plain-text password
  • WhatsApp number — collected during registration to verify your identity and help prevent fake or duplicate account creation. This number may also be used in a future update to send you a One-Time Password (OTP) for account verification
  • Profile information you optionally provide during onboarding
📌 WhatsApp Number & Future OTP Verification: We currently collect your WhatsApp number at sign-up solely to verify that you are a genuine user and to prevent fake or duplicate account creation. We do not currently send OTP messages to this number. In a future app update, we may begin using it to deliver a One-Time Password (OTP) for account verification — if we do, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify you in-app beforehand. We do not use your WhatsApp number for marketing or promotional messages, and it is never sold or shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

1.2 — Device & Licensing Data

  • Device model and OS version — for app compatibility
  • Unique device identifier — for subscription license enforcement (to prevent account sharing)
  • App version — for update management and bug tracking
  • IP address — collected automatically when you register or use our services, used for account security, fraud/duplicate-account detection, and to comply with legal obligations

1.3 — Subscription & Payment Data

  • Your current subscription plan and status (active, expired, trial)
  • Payment transaction IDs and dates (processed by Google Play — we do not store card or bank details)
  • EA license status and download history

1.4 — Signal & App Usage Data

  • Which signals you viewed in the app (signal ID, timestamp) — used to improve content relevance and fix technical issues
  • In-app navigation patterns and feature usage — for product improvement
  • App crash reports and error logs — for technical debugging

1.5 — Push Notification Data

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token — used to send you signal alerts, subscription reminders, and important announcements

1.6 — Support Data

  • Messages and attachments you send through in-app live chat or email support

1.7 — Profile Photo (Optional)

  • Profile photo — if you choose to personalize your profile, you can take a new photo with your camera, choose an existing photo from your device's gallery, or pick one of our built-in illustrated avatars instead
  • If you take or choose a photo, it is automatically resized/compressed on your device and any location (GPS) or other device metadata (EXIF data) embedded in the photo is stripped out before it is uploaded
  • Uploaded photos are stored on our own servers — never shared with, or stored by, any third party or advertiser
  • Built-in illustrated avatars are drawn entirely within the app itself; choosing one does not upload or transmit any photo or image at all
  • Your profile photo is visible only to you, and to our support team if you contact support (so we can identify your account) — it is never shown publicly to other users of the app
  • You can change or permanently remove your profile photo at any time from Profile → Edit Profile
📌 Camera & Photo Gallery Permission: If you choose to take or upload a photo, your device will ask you to grant camera or photo gallery access. This permission is used solely so you can capture or select your profile photo — we do not access your camera or gallery at any other time or for any other purpose, and adding a profile photo is entirely optional.
📌 What the EA Does NOT Send to Us: The Expert Advisor (EA) software, when running on your device, connects outbound to our API solely to receive signal data. The EA does not transmit your broker credentials, account balance, trade history, open positions, profit/loss data, or any other information from your trading environment back to Trading Insights. We have zero visibility into your broker account at any time.

4️⃣ Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable and industry-standard steps to protect your personal data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are stored as cryptographic hashes — we cannot view or recover your plain-text password
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict all unauthorized read/write access
  • API authentication requires signed JWT Bearer tokens for all requests
  • Device registration limits unauthorized account access
  • Server access is restricted to authorized personnel only
⚠️ No system is completely immune to security risks. While we implement strong safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account password and for any activity under your account.
